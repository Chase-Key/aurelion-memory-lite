## üèóÔ∏è The 5-Floor Architecture

Knowledge is organized into 5 logical floors based on abstraction level and use frequency:

### **FLOOR 1: Foundation & Navigation**
*Purpose: Operational knowledge, daily work, career tracking*

**Category: Entry Point & Daily Operations**
- Hub Index (navigation dashboard, quick links)
- Career Master (career timeline, goals, advancement)
- Skills Inventory (capabilities, proficiency tracking)
- Daily Operations (recurring tasks, workflows)
- Current Projects (active work, assignments)
- Past Projects (case studies, reference examples)
- Quality Standards (criteria, best practices)
  
**Use Case:** Day-to-day operations, career management, skill tracking

---

### **FLOOR 2: Systems & Understanding**
*Purpose: Frameworks, processes, reusable templates*

**Category: Knowledge Frameworks & Standards**
- Glossary (terminology, acronyms, definitions)
- Communication Standards (templates, formats)
- Standard Operating Procedures (documented processes)
- Training Materials (how-to guides)
- Methodology Documentation (approaches, frameworks)
  
**Use Case:** Systematizing knowledge, creating reusable processes

---

### **FLOOR 3: Networks & Relationships**
*Purpose: Stakeholder mapping, strategic positioning*

**Category: Professional Relationships & Strategy**
- Stakeholder Map (relationships, communication strategies)
- Professional Vision (12-month roadmap, goals)
- Career Narrative (professional story, positioning)
- Organizational Dynamics (team structure, influence)
- Networking Strategy (relationship building)

**Use Case:** Strategic planning, career advancement, stakeholder management

---

### **FLOOR 4: Action & Implementation**
*Purpose: Tactical execution, domain-specific work*

**Category: Execution Frameworks & Playbooks**
- Project Templates (reusable methodologies)
- Decision Trees (when to escalate, what to investigate)
- Tactical Playbooks (step-by-step execution)
- Training Curricula (knowledge transfer)
- Process Documentation (implementation guides)

**Use Case:** Executing projects, training others, tactical work

---

### **FLOOR 5: Vision & Meaning**
*Purpose: Long-term thinking, personal philosophy*

**Category: Strategic Direction & Philosophy**
- Long-term Vision (3-5 year goals)
- Personal Philosophy (thinking patterns, values)
- Strategic Scenarios (contingency planning)
- Leadership Framework (management style, principles)
- Future Planning (career trajectory, aspirations)

**Use Case:** Long-term planning, philosophical reflection, strategic direction

## üó∫Ô∏è Navigation & Search

### **Finding Information**

**Search Methods:**
1. **By Topic** - Search for specific subjects or keywords
2. **By Floor** - Browse by knowledge category (1-5)
3. **By Tag** - Filter by purpose or type
4. **By Date** - Access session notes chronologically
5. **By Connection** - Follow links between related documents

**Benefits:**
- Multiple paths to same information
- Related documents linked together
- Intuitive organization once learned
- Supports discovery and pattern recognition
